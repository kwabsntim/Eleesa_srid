Welcome to the Electrical Engineering Students Association,Srid (ELEESA)!

We are thrilled to have you here! Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of electrical engineers.

Through exciting events, hands-on projects, and engaging discussions, we aim to create an environment where students can grow academically and professionally.
